Snow White beckons you to enter her retreat in the woods. This forest is alive with magic! Hidden among the flowers and trees are the Seven Dwarfs and other characters from the classic Disney film.


Snow White's Magical Forest portrays the lovely princess in the forest that is the home of the seven dwarfs. This enchanted forest holds ten surprises that can be discovered by a youthful heart and a keen eye. Concealed in the leafy boughs, nooks and crannies are Doc, Bashful, Happy, Sneezy, Grumpy and of course, Dopey.


This Tom duBois print is from the Disney Discovery Collection from 1996. It is signed and numbered and comes complete with a Certificate of Authenticity. This DELUXE EDITION print is #190 of 495. It is flat, not rolled, and comes in a cardboard sleeve with a picture of the print on the sleeve also. It measures 26 X 31 with a 1.5 inch white border.
