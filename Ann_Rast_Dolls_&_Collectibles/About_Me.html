About Me

 

I’ve been collecting dolls for nearly 40 years focusing on vintage Madame Alexander dolls and, more recently, Robert Tonner fashion dolls.  I’ve been a member of the Madame Alexander Doll Club since 1986 and am a founding member of the Robert Tonner Collectors Club.  I am currently the Volunteer Coordinator for Tonner Doll Conventions.


Many will remember the years I made custom doll stands for Madame Alexander dolls and sold them through many, many doll shops throughout the US, department stores such as I. Magnin, Horchows, Saks, Robertsons, Disney World, MGM Grand Las Vegas, MADC Conventions, Collectors United, as well as through large circulation catalogs such as Lillian Vernon.


I’ve reached a point where I would like to downsize and do a lot of things I’ve not had time to do in the past.  Other interests have also become important to me.  So, I have decided to sell most, if not all, of my extensive doll collection.  Please feel free to contact me for further information or if I can help you in some way.
