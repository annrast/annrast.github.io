Doll Stand Sleeves

 

These are all leftover “sleeves” for custom doll stands I made in the 90s--I have hundreds, if not thousands, of them.  They fit on the upright back of an 8 or 10-inch dollstand and are approximately 9-10-inches in length.  These sleeves matched or coordinated with 8-10-inch Madame Alexander dolls of that period.  Stands were sold through department stores, catalogs, doll shops, and shows during that time:   I. Magnin, Horchows, Lillian Vernon, MGM Grand, Disney, Warner Bros, & hundreds of doll shops.

Prices are $.25 each sleeve in bundles of 25-27--Sold in bundles only except for the Misc colors directly below.  Larger pictures available by email.
