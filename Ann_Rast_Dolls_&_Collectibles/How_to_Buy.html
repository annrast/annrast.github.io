How to Purchase:

Forms of payment:


PERSONAL CHECK or US POST OFFICE MONEY ORDER:

I prefer a personal check or U.S. Post Office Money Order.  Personal checks take 10-12 days to physically clear the bank.  While checks clear rather quickly electronically, it takes 10-12 days to actually get to the bank.


PAYPAL:

If you wish to use Paypal, that is acceptable as well.  Please add 2.9% to your total if you do use Paypal.


LAYAWAY:

Layaway is available for those who would like to use it.  I can structure payments to meet your needs.  Most buyers like a 2 or 3-part layaway but most anything you need can be worked out.


SHIPPING:

All prices shown do not include shipping cost.  I typically will use FedEx Ground as it is the most cost effective for expensive items.  For less expensive items, USPS Priority Mail or First Class Mail may be used.
